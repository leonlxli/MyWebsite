Itz Lit

MINIMIZE YOUR WORRIES BY MAXIMIZING YOUR SAFETY ON YOUR COMMUTES AFTER DARK.

Itz Lit is an application that help people in San Diego feel safer while traveling at night, by analyzing San Diego’s crimes, street lights, home and route data. The routes provided will take into account your start and ending destinations. You can pick whether or not it is worth it to utilize that route based on the amount of light and crimes near that route in comparison to other routes provided. You can chat with other people from San Diego, to let each other know what's going on out there!

https://itzlit.herokuapp.com/

Tech Used

  • React
  • MongoDB
  • Socket.io
  • D3.js
  • Express

Youber

HELPING UBER DRIVERS MAXIMIZE THEIR SUCCESS.

Youber is an application that helps Uber drivers minimize their wait time by analyzing each region's population density, income, family size, vehicle availability and ethnicity, as well as current events.
